# Services - How Working Together Works

## Pricing

**Email me for rates.** Sessions are typically short working calls over Google Meet (via Calendly).

No multi-year packages. Book what you need. A lot of families start with one session and continue only if it's useful.

---

## Who it’s for

- Bay Area high school students and parents (tech-family friendly, plain language) 
- Students exploring **UC-focused** paths: first-year or transfer 
- Anyone using dual enrollment, AP, early graduation, or TAG and wanting a coherent plan 

---

## What a session is good for

### 1. Major and campus strategy
- Match interests + transcript + selectivity 
- Decide between related majors for admissions positioning 
- Build a shortlist with reach / target / likely logic 

### 2. Dual enrollment + AP planning
- Which courses to take next term at a CCC 
- How to combine AP and dual enrollment without overload 
- What increases UC competitiveness while staying **doable** 

### 3. Four-year (or 2+2) roadmap
- Term-by-term sketch for 9th–12th or CCC → UC 
- Early graduation feasibility 
- IGETC vs major-prep sequencing 

### 4. TAG and transfer execution
- Whether TAG fits 
- assist.org major prep gaps 
- Application season checklist (PIQ topics, activities framing - guidance, not ghostwriting) 

### 5. Parent alignment
- Translate counselor-speak into a family decision 
- Document questions for the school / CCC 

---

## What I don’t do

- Guarantee admission to any campus 
- Write PIQs or personal statements for you 
- Replace official high school, CCC, or UC advisors 
- Provide licensed financial-aid packaging (I can only point to official tools) 
- Multi-year locked retainers (unless we later agree on simple follow-ups)

## Philosophy

The goal is a plan you can execute during a real school week - sports, sleep, family, and sanity included. Maximum prestige with a burned-out transcript is a bad product. **Optimal and doable** beats **theoretical perfect**.
